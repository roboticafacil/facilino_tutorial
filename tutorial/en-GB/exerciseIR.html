Infrared Remote (IR)

Learn how to receive IR codes from your IR remote controller to command 'orders' to your device.

Required material.
  • Arduino Uno (and compatible) or Wemos D1R2/D1R32.
  • Arduino multisensor shield.
  • IR controller.

Additional instructions
Exercises
  1. IR Codes

    Print the received InfraRed Codes on the console to get an overview of the communications channel.

  2. IR 'Piano'

    Generate a music tone based on the InfraRed Signal received to create a remotely controlled piano.

    Hint:

    In the block Advanced Buzzer you already have a function inluded, that converts number signal into half tone distances.

  3. IR Commands

    Set the color of an RGB LED based on the received InfraRed Code.

    Hint:

    Combine the Knowledge gained from the previous RGB Exercise with IR Commands.
