LM35 Temperature Sensor

The LM35 is an analog Temperature Sensor. It has a linear response with a resolution of 10mV per Celsius Degree. Around 25°C it has the highest Precision with a accuracy of 0.5°C, its range is -55°C to 150°C. It has three Pins, one connected to Ground, one connected to 5V Supply and the last one for the analog signal.
