Light Dimmer

Learn how to regulate (dimm) light brightness of a AC LED lamp using a light dimmer (with triacs and zero-crossing detector) with Facilino.

Required material.
  • Arduino Uno (and compatible) or Wemos D1R2/D1R32.
  • Arduino multisensor shield.
