RGB LED Strips

Learn how to set colors of RGB LED strips individually or in group. Also learn how to set the LEDs brightness.

Required material.
  • Arduino Uno (and compatible) or Wemos D1R2/D1R32.
  • RGB LED strip.
Worked instructions
Additional instructions
Exercises
  1. RGB LED Strip

    Set the first LED in the Strip to Red, the second LED to Green and the third LED to Blue. Control the whole LED Strip with one Digital Pin.

  2. RGB LED Strip Brightness

    Set the first LED again to Red, the second LED to Green and the third LED to Blue. Increase the brightness of the LEDs from 0% to 100% in a loop.

    Hint:

    Create a loop, with an increment from 1 to 100 representing the intensity.

  3. RGB LED Strip Inline

    Shift the color combination of Red, Green and Blue with 3 LEDs, continous to the right in a loop.

    Hint:

    View the moving LEDs as three different setting on the strip.
